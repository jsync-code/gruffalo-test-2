Eve & Olivia's Guide To Spotting a Gruffalo

  1. He has terrible tusks, and terrible claws, and terrible teeth in his terrible jaws.
  2. He has knobbly knees, and turned-out toes, and a poisonous wart at the end of his nose.
  3. His eyes are orange, his tongue is black, he has purple prickles all over his back.
